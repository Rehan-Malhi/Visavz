1. How long does it take to process a visa?

Visa processing time varies by country and visa type. On average, visit and study visas take 2–6 weeks, while immigration and work visas may take longer.

2. Do you guarantee visa approval?

No one can guarantee approval, but we ensure your documents, application, and process are fully optimized to maximize your chances.

3. Can I apply for a visa without IELTS?

Yes, some countries and visa types allow alternatives to IELTS. We’ll help you explore options based on your profile and destination.

4. How much do your services cost?

Our consultation is free. The service fee depends on the visa type and complexity. We’ll provide a clear quote before starting the process.
